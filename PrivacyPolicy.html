Privacy Policy for HRA Coin

At HRA Coin, we take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services. By using our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

THE

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you sign up for an HRA Coin account, we may collect the following data:

  • - Name
  • - Email address
  • - Phone number
  • - Payment information (including cryptocurrency wallet details)
  • - Identity verification
    (including KYC information such as passport, driver's license, or other identification documents)

Automatically Collected Information

When you use our website or app, we automatically collect information such as:

  • - IP address
  • - Browser and device information
  • - Usage data, including timestamps and interactions with our services

Transaction Data

We collect data about your transactions within the HRA Experience ecosystem, including payments you make using HRA Coin for flights, hotels, and other services.

AND

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

- Account Management: To enable you to create, use, and manage your account.

- Transaction Processing: To process payments and transactions with HRA Coin within the HRA Experience ecosystem.

- Communication: For customer support and providing updates about our services.

- Security and KYC (Know Your Customer): For identity verification and to comply with legal requirements.

- Marketing: To send you relevant offers and updates unless you opt out of receiving marketing communications.

Sharing Your Information

We only share your information with third parties in the following cases:

- Service Providers:To perform necessary services such as payment processing, cloud storage, and customer support.

- Legal Requirements:When required by law or to comply with requests from regulatory authorities.

- Acquisition or Merger:If HRA Coin is acquired or merges with another company, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

How we Protect Your Information

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include:

- Encryption of data during transmission and storage

- Regular security audits and tests

- Access control based on privileges

Your Rights

As a user of HRA Coin, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- Access:You can request access to the data we have collected about you.

- Correction:You can ask us to correct any inaccurate or outdated information.

- Deletion:You can request that we delete your data, provided this does not conflict with our legal obligations.

- Objection:You can object to the use of your data for specific purposes, such as marketing.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Data Transfer

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside of your country of residence, where different data protection rules may apply. We ensure that any international data transfer complies with applicable laws.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

HRA Coin reserves the right to change this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and, where necessary, we will notify you by email or through a notice within our services. It is your responsibility to review this policy regularly.
